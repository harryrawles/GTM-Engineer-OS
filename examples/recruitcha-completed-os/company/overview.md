## What They Do

Recruitcha places vetted software engineers (fullstack, backend, AI/ML) into US VC-backed SaaS startups. They source exclusively from other VC-backed product companies — not outsourcing firms or IT consultancies. Handles onboarding, payroll, and international compliance. Success-only pricing, no upfront retainers.

## Why They Hired Instantly.ai

Drew and Harrison built the agency on referrals and outbound. Growth plateaued in early 2026. They tried 2 internal SDRs but reply rates were under 1%. Came to Instantly.ai for systematic, signal-based outbound. Goal: 8-12 qualified meetings per month from cold.

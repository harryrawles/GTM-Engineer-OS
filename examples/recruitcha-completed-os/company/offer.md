# Offer — Recruitcha (Example)

## The Offer in One Line

> Place a vetted senior engineer from a VC-backed product company in your team within 72 hours — success-only, no retainer.

## What They Sell

**Product / service:** Technical recruitment — senior engineering placements (fullstack, backend, AI/ML)
**Category:** Specialist recruitment agency for US SaaS
**Delivery model:** Success-only pricing — no fee unless a hire is made
**Pricing:** 18-22% of first-year base salary, paid 30 days after placement

## The Outcome Promise

**Big claim:** Shortlist of 3 vetted candidates from VC-backed product companies within 72 hours
**Timeframe:** 72 hours to shortlist, 21 days to placement (92% of roles)
**Guarantee / risk reversal:** No fee unless a hire is made + 90-day replacement guarantee on direct hires

## Proof Points

| Proof type | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Case study 1 | Saved TurboTenant $100k+ in hiring costs across 4 senior engineer placements (verified) |
| Case study 2 | Saved HealthNote / Homeroom $80k+ across 3 placements |
| Case study 3 | LSAT Demon — $120k saved, 2 senior engineers placed in 18 days |
| Aggregate stat | 92% of roles filled within 21 days |
| Aggregate stat | 72-hour shortlist guarantee, 70+ funded startups placed with |
| Volume stat | 100+ engineer placements in 2025 |

**Approved client logos for copy use:** TurboTenant, HealthNote, Homeroom, LSAT Demon, Crescent, Proxis.ai (others require check before use).

## Key Differentiators

1. **Sources only from VC-backed product companies** — not outsourcing firms, not consultancies. "Apples to apples" candidate quality.
2. **Pre-vetted before they reach your pipeline** — no early-stage filtering needed by client.
3. **Success-only model** — no fees unless a hire is made. Aligns incentives.
4. **International compliance and payroll handled** — relevant for distributed teams.
5. **72-hour shortlist + 21-day close average** — fastest in the segment.

## The 3 Value Props (rotating across emails)

| Value prop | Recruitcha angle |
|-----------|------------------|
| Make money | Faster placements → faster engineer productivity → faster product velocity → faster revenue |
| Save time | 72-hour shortlist vs 6-week average. Senior engineers stop losing 10-20 hrs/week interviewing low-signal candidates. |
| Save money | 40-70% cost saving vs local US hires. Success-only = no retainer waste. |

## Positioning

**Against the market:** "The agency that only sources from VC-backed product companies" — owns the "candidate provenance" category. Competes with Greenhouse search products and traditional staffing.

**Who they are not for:**
- Companies under 1 engineer (no team to onboard into)
- Companies hiring junior / mid-level (Recruitcha is senior-only)
- Companies outside US (compliance overhead too high)
- Companies wanting purely contract / temp (Recruitcha is direct hire only)

## Offer Notes

- "Apples to apples" is a phrase Drew uses frequently — has tested well in copy.
- Avoid "vetted" without qualification — feels generic. Always pair with "from VC-backed product companies" to be specific.
- The $100k TurboTenant figure is the strongest single proof point. Use in Email 1 of every hiring signal campaign.
- "90-day replacement guarantee" is rarely needed in copy because risk reversal is already covered by "no hire, no fee." Save for objection handling.

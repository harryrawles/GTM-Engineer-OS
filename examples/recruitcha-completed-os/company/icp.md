# ICP — Recruitcha (Example)

## Target Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Company type | VC-backed SaaS / product-led startups |
| Company size | 1-200 employees, Series A through Series C |
| Industries | SaaS, Software Development, Information Technology, Internet |
| Geography | US — primarily SF Bay Area, NYC, Boston, Houston, Dallas |
| Decision-maker titles | See priority order below |
| Signals to target | See signal priority table |

## Signal Priority

| Signal | Source | Priority | Why it works |
|--------|--------|----------|-------------|
| Hiring 3+ engineering roles in 30 days | LinkedIn Jobs + PredictLeads | 1 — highest PRR (~2.3%) | Active need, urgency, budget present |
| Series A-C funding 30-90 days ago | Crunchbase + Intellizence | 2 (~1.6% PRR) | New budget, scaling pressure |
| Champion job change (former candidate at new company) | Clay + Champify | 3 (warm — small volume) | Warm relationship |

Only contact prospects when a signal is present. Recent test (T-004 in test-log.md) confirmed no-signal control hit 0.4% PRR vs 2.3% for hiring signal.

## Avoid List

| Exclusion | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| Staffing and recruiting firms | Competitors |
| IT services / consulting companies | Sells to enterprise, not Recruitcha's category |
| Crypto / blockchain startups | High failure rate, payment risk |
| Companies under 1 employee or no LinkedIn page | Likely stealth or shell |
| Companies that hired internal Talent Lead in last 30 days | Already filled the need |

## Decision-Maker Notes

| Title | What they care about | Copy angle (ATL/BTL) |
|-------|---------------------|----------------------|
| CEO / Co-Founder | Hiring velocity, cost-to-hire, runway impact | ATL — strategic. "Headcount growth without runway hit" |
| CTO | Engineering velocity, hire quality, retention | ATL — strategic. "Senior engineers from product companies" |
| VP Engineering | Time-to-productive, candidate quality, ramp time | BTL — operational. "92% filled in 21 days, 72-hour shortlists" |
| Head of People / Chief People Officer | Process efficiency, candidate experience, compliance | BTL — operational. "International compliance handled end-to-end" |

Avoid: HR Managers (no budget authority), Talent Acquisition Specialists (too junior).

## Lead Filters (for Instantly / lead sourcing)

```
Company type: VC-backed SaaS, product startups
Headcount: 10-200
Industry: SaaS, Software Development, Information Technology, Internet
Geography: US (SF, NYC, Boston, Houston, Dallas, Seattle, LA)
Title includes: Founder, Co-Founder, CEO, CTO, Head of Engineering, VP Engineering, Head of HR, VP People, Chief People Officer
Title excludes: HR Manager, Talent Acquisition Specialist, Recruiter, Talent Sourcer
Funding stage: Series A, Series B, Series C (within last 24 months)
Technology: AWS / GCP / Azure (signals real product-led tech)
Avoid technologies: Crypto-specific stacks (Solana, Ethereum SDKs, etc.)
```

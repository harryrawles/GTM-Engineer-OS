# Playbook: SaaS — Vertical Automation / Real-Time Pricing

**Type:** Shared campaign playbook (client-agnostic). Reusable for any client selling a **purpose-built,
plug-and-play SaaS automation platform** into a fast-moving vertical — led by operational risk + speed,
"industry-specific not generic", technical readiness, and a low-friction free trial.
**Origin:** Abstracted from a precious-metals pricing-automation SaaS campaign. All brand-specific values are
placeholders — fill them from the active client's `clients/{slug}/offer.md`, `icp.md`, and `voice.md`.

> **How to use:** this is a starting structure, not drop-in copy. Swap every `{{PLACEHOLDER}}` for the
> active client's real values, and only make claims that appear in that client's `voice.md` → *Approved
> Claims*. Named customers (`{{NOTABLE_CUSTOMERS}}`) require permission to reference — use only approved
> logos. Trial terms (`{{TRIAL_OFFER}}`) and integration claims (APIs, ERP sync) must be real. The copy below
> is written around precious-metals dealers — re-skin the vertical, pain points, and integrations to the
> client's actual market. Never carry one client's specifics into another.

## Campaign Goal

Engage operators in a volatile / fast-moving vertical by addressing the **operational risk of manual work**
during market swings, and position the client's platform as a **purpose-built, plug-and-play** automation
solution for that industry.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Targets (example — precious-metals dealers; re-skin per client):**
- Online dealers / ecommerce operators in the vertical
- Operations / product managers
- CTOs / technical leads
- Finance or pricing owners

**Company types:**
- Ecommerce operators in the niche
- Multi-channel sellers (website + marketplaces)
- Businesses managing large, fast-moving catalogs

**Pain points:**
- Manual updates during fast market swings
- Margin risk from delayed action
- Complex integrations with data/dealer feeds
- Sync issues across ecommerce, ERP, and marketplaces
- Building and maintaining infrastructure in-house

## Core Messaging Strategy

- Lead with **risk + speed** (the market moves fast, so must the ops)
- Emphasise **automation over manual work**
- Highlight the **industry-specific build** (not generic software)
- Reinforce **technical readiness** (APIs, live feeds, ERP sync)
- Offer a **free trial** (`{{TRIAL_OFFER}}`) to lower adoption friction
- Tone: operational, confident, pragmatic, technical-but-clear (defer to `clients/{slug}/voice.md`)

## Key Selling Points (adapt to the client's real, approved capabilities)

- Real-time automation of the core, volatile task
- Native API integrations (dealer feeds, exchanges)
- Live cost / data syncing
- Multi-channel catalog accuracy
- No manual work
- Purpose-built for the vertical (not generic)
- Free trial with full support (`{{TRIAL_OFFER}}`)

## When to Use This Playbook

**Use when:**
- Targeting operators in a volatile, fast-moving vertical
- The market/data moves fast and manual work is the pain
- Operations teams are overwhelmed
- You want technical buyers engaged

**Avoid when:**
- Targeting static-pricing / non-commodity businesses
- Teams without API readiness
- Verticals where the "real-time" urgency doesn't apply

## Why This Works

- Directly addresses margin / operational risk
- Highly vertical-specific
- Speaks to ops + tech buyers
- Reduces fear of implementation
- Free trial removes friction

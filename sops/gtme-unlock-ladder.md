# SOP: GTME Unlock Ladder

When: you're stuck on a client. Campaigns aren't moving, results aren't improving, and you don't know the
next step.

Work through this ladder in order. These three resources exist so you can solve problems at the lowest
level possible. Use Loom and Cowork to show what you're stuck on, written and visual beats a call every
time.

---

## Step 1 - Query Cowork

Before anything else, ask Cowork. Describe the client situation, the campaign performance, and what
you've already tried.

Be specific:
- What's the PRR?
- What copy and ICP have been tested?
- What's the deliverability looking like?
- What have you already tried, and why didn't it work?

If Cowork gives you a clear direction, execute it. No need to go further.

---

## Step 2 - Query Your Support Channel

Still stuck? Post in your support channel. Drop a Loom walking through:
- The client's situation and goal
- What's been tried
- Where you're stuck
- What you think the next step might be, even if you're not sure

A Loom beats a call every time. It gives the team full context without pulling everyone into a meeting.

---

## Step 3 - Query #grill-my-campaigns

Post the campaign in #grill-my-campaigns for a peer review. Include:
- The copy
- The ICP and targeting
- Current performance data
- What you want feedback on

Again, use a Loom if it helps explain the context. Written plus visual beats a call.

---

## The Goal

Solve it at the lowest level possible. Steps 1-3 are your resources, use all three before anything else.
Every problem solved here is a call that never needs to happen.

---

## Where this fits in the OS

- **This is the escalation path named in `sops/campaign-performance-standards.md`.** At 1,000+ sends per
  positive reply (PRR under 0.1%), that SOP says escalate immediately, this ladder is how: Cowork, then
  the support channel, then `#grill-my-campaigns`, before it becomes a call or a manager escalation.
- **This is not the same escalation as `sops/am-gtme-responsibility-split.md`.** That document is about who
  owns a task (AM, CSM, manager). This ladder is about what to do when the task is still GTME's, the
  campaign is stuck, and you need help solving it rather than handing it off.
- **`gtm-skills/campaign-optimiser.md`** reads this SOP when its own diagnosis stalls, working the ladder
  comes before defaulting to a call with Harry's manager.
- **This is Step 3 of `sops/client-not-getting-results.md`.** When results are underperforming and a
  client conversation is coming, work this ladder before that conversation, not after.
